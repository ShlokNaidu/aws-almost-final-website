About AWS Cloud Club

Nurturing cloud leaders of tomorrow

Our Mission

In Madhya Pradesh, there are only three active AWS Cloud Clubs, and we're proud to be one of them at MediCaps University. We provide a supportive environment where participants can engage with experts, share experiences, and work on real-world projects.

What We Do

Our club's activities include conducting sessions, seminars, workshops, competitions, and events to foster student learning and encourage exploration to enhance understanding and broaden horizons in cloud computing.

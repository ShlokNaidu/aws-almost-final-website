About AWS Cloud Club

We're a community-driven organization dedicated to fostering cloud computing excellence and preparing students for successful careers in the cloud industry.

Our Mission

To democratize cloud education and create a pathway for students to become cloud-ready professionals through hands-on learning, industry partnerships, and real-world project experience.

Our Vision

To be the leading student organization that bridges the gap between academic learning and industry requirements in cloud computing, producing job-ready cloud professionals.

AWS Certifications

Get comprehensive preparation for AWS certification exams with our structured study groups, practice tests, and expert guidance. We've helped over 200 students achieve their AWS certifications.
